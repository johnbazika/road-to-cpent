## 👋 About Me

I'm **John Bazika Bulambo** — known as **Coach John** — a cybersecurity professional based in **Kinshasa, Democratic Republic of the Congo**, and founder of **[D-CORP Academy](https://dcorpacademy.com)**, an official EC-Council & Cisco Networking Academy partner.

My credentials: **CPENT** (88.4%), **CEH Master v13**, **CEH Practical**, **CCNP**, and a **Master's in Cybersecurity (Cum Laude)** from EC-Council University.

I built everything from the DRC — without relocating — and today I train the next generation of French-speaking cybersecurity professionals across Africa and beyond.

> This repository documents **how I prepared for the CPENT**, the methodology I follow, the tools I rely on, and the lessons I learned along the way. It's meant as both a **portfolio** and a **resource for those walking the same path.**

---

## 🎯 What This Repository Is (and Isn't)

**✅ What you'll find here:**
- My penetration testing **methodology** and mindset
- **Tool references** and how I use them in a professional workflow
- **Study strategy** and preparation roadmap for offensive security certifications
- **Note-taking templates** I use to stay organized during engagements
- Curated **learning resources**

**🚫 What you will NOT find here:**
- Any exam content, leaked questions, or solutions (CPENT is under NDA — I respect it, and so should you)
- Step-by-step exploitation of real targets without authorization
- Anything that isn't legal, ethical, and educational

> ⚠️ **Ethical use only.** Everything here is for learning and authorized testing. Never test systems you don't own or don't have explicit written permission to assess.
